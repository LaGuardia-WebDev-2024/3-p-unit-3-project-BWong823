Soup Dumplings

  • Time: Around 3 hours
  • Serves: 12 dumplings a serving

Ingredients Quantity
Broth
  • Chicken
  • Pork
    		•  Around a small pot amount
    Dumpling Wrappers Around a dozen, but it is how ever many you prefer

    Step 1: Best quality broth is cooked from bones and created fresh in a congealed-like form, but if you find pork broth in the store, use it.

    Step 2: Using that broth, you wrap the congeal broth into the dumpling as well as any other ingriedients like pork or any vegetables

    Step 3: Then, simmer the dumplings on a stove top. The gelatin like soup will then melt down and become the soupy part of the filling.

    Step 4: This is optional, but for the real soup dumpling experience, you need to have a sauce to go with it. You want to add garlic, green onions, soy sauce, Chinese black vinegar (Chinkiang Vinegar) and chili oil
